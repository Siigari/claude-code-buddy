# Contributing to Claude Code Buddy

Thanks for your interest! This guide is based on patterns developed over 200+ real sessions. Contributions that extend or refine these patterns are welcome.

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork** this repo
2. **Create a branch** for your change
3. **Submit a PR** with a clear description of what you're adding or changing

## What's Welcome

- Additional patterns or techniques you've discovered
- Corrections or clarifications to existing sections
- Real-world examples of the system in action
- Translations

## What's Not

- Promotional content or links to paid products
- Patterns that require third-party plugins or paid services (the whole point is that this works with just files)
- AI-generated filler content
